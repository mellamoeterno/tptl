about: setOpen(prevOpen => !prevOpen)

Everything to the left of => is the function’s input (parameters),
           and everything to the right => is what the function returns

“When you call setOpen you give it a little instruction: 

Take the current value (prevOpen), then with the right side (!prevOpen)
invert the current value:
(if true → false, if false → true), and use that as the new value.”

in other words

whats on the left of the => is the earlier version, and whats 
on the right will be replacing the left side
